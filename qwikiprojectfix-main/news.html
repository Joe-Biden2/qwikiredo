QUAKE NEWS

Quakecon was held this year between August 8-11 at the Gaylord Texas Convention Center! While there wasn't as much news on Quake itself, the event did talk a lot about modding for other ID Software games... no new Quake game though.


NEW QDQ

In the prominent speedrunning scene, a new installment in the collaberative Quake Done Quick has released, Quake Done Double Quickest Lite! This time, the speedrun uses multiplayer (thus the "Double Quickest") The full run can be viewed in the side bar - it's a great watch even if you're not a speedrunner yourself.


SICK NEW MODS

In other news, a new Quake mod has released, called Duake - it combines the weapons of Doom 1 and 2 with the level design of Quake, making for an extremely fun and fresh experience for returning players! Check it out Here, I promise it's worth a look!
