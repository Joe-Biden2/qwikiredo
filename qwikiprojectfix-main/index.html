WHAT IS QUAKE

Quake is a first person shooter (FPS) by ID Software, the creators of Wolfenstein 3D and most prominately the Doom FPS games (FPS is kind of their specialty). It's known for its fast paced gameplay and incredible atmosphere.


WHY QUAKE?

This game has had a monumental impact on the FPS genre and gaming as a whole, as its method of rendering polygons for a 3D space is to this day the most efficient way that has been found, along with the game showing that atmosphere can be a part of a video game's identity, something that was looked down upon in the past.
